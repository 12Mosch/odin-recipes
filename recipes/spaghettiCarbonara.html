Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces spaghetti
  • 4 ounces pancetta, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. In a pan, cook pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and Parmesan in a bowl.
  4. Drain pasta, reserving Â½ cup cooking water.
  5. Toss pasta with pancetta off heat, then quickly stir in egg-cheese mixture, adding water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Season with lots of black pepper and serve immediately.
